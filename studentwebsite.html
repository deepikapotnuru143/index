VISION & MISSION

VISION

To lead in Data Science education and research, empowering students to innovate, solve problems, and make impactful decisions using data-driven technologies for the benefit of society and industry.

MISSION

M1: Provide a strong foundation in data science for innovative thinking and problem solving.
M2: Incorporate practical skills in database design, implementation, and decision support systems to enhance business processes.
M3: Prepare students in creating effective data models, maintain data quality, gain insights, and manage large datasets.
